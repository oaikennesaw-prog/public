Part B · Core Principles Defined & Article Matching

Core Principles Defined

  • Popular Sovereignty: Authority from consent of the governed.
    Hint: Often linked to Articles V & VII.
  • Limited Government: Power restricted by law and enumerated powers.
    Hint: Often linked to Articles I & II.
  • Separation of Powers: Legislative (makes), Executive (enforces), Judicial (interprets).
    Hint: Often linked to Articles I, II & III.
  • Checks & Balances: Branches limit one another.
    Hint: Often linked to Articles I, II & III.
  • Federalism: Power shared national/state; federal law supreme.
    Hint: Often linked to Articles IV & VI.
  • Republicanism: People elect representatives.
    Hint: Often linked to Articles I & IV.
  • Individual Rights: Fundamental freedoms protected.
    Hint: Often linked to Article VI (no religious test).
